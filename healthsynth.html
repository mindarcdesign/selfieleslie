Selfie Leslie faces a significant mobile-desktop performance gap. Desktop scores average 62/100 with sub-3-second LCP, while mobile users experience scores of 34-38/100 with LCP times reaching 10-17 seconds under throttled conditions. The root cause is identifiable: LCP images are lazy-loaded on the PLP, and a heavy third-party script stack (12+ scripts including Yotpo, Clarity, Searchspring, Klaviyo) blocks the main thread for 860-1213ms. The good news: server response times are excellent (27-40ms), accessibility scores are strong (76-93%), and the underlying infrastructure is sound ‚Äî making targeted optimizations highly achievable.
